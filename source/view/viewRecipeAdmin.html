1001 Spaghetti Carbonara Spaghetti (200g), Pancetta or bacon (100g), Eggs (2), Parmesan cheese (50g), Black pepper 1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
2. In a pan, cook pancetta or bacon until crispy. Drain excess fat.
3. In a bowl, whisk eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
4. Drain cooked spaghetti and toss in the egg mixture until coated.
5. Add cooked pancetta or bacon and mix well. Serve immediately. 		20 mins Italian Lunch 450 None url
